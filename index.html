About

We have various best seller books, one of which is tere liye's book. Some of Tere Liye's works that became best sellers include: 1. "Bumi" (Bumi Series): The Bumi series is Tere Liye's epic work consisting of several books, among which "Bumi" has become very popular among readers. The series tells the story of the adventures of the main character, Bumi, in facing various challenges. 2. "Hafalan Shalat Delisa": This novel gained great attention after it was made into a movie in 2011. The story is based on the true story of Delisa, a girl who lost her family in the earthquake in Aceh and found peace in worship.

3. "Ayahku (Bukan) Pembohong": This book tells a family story, depicting the relationship between a father and son. The book touched the hearts of many readers as it promotes family values and honesty. 4. "Negeri Para Bedebah": This novel features the friendship and adventures of four young boys in a village called Bedebah. The story is full of humor and also provides moral messages.

So, happy browsing through our book catalog that is rich in stories and information.
